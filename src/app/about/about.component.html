About me

Hi, I'm Louisa, a creative and tech-savvy individual with a solid foundation in graphic and object design, as well as experience as a media manager in digital and print. After an intense immersion into the world of computer visualisation and design, and four successful years as a digital business manager, I am currently in the midst of an exciting training course to become a full-stack developer at the Developer Academy. These steps reflect my deep affinity for innovation, design, technology and art.

My career has been characterised by a constant search for new challenges and a passion for combining technological solutions with creative design. As someone who is at home at the intersection of computer science and creative design, I strive to create projects that are not only technically sophisticated, but also aesthetically pleasing. Immerse yourself in my projects and discover how I turn my visions into reality. Thank you for visiting!
